rules

NO NSFW OBVIOUSLY

None of us like NSFW, and we want to keep it that way. Don't post anything inappropriate that you wouldn't show to your parents.

DON'T ABUSE SWEARING

Swearing is fine, but don't overuse it. Some people may be uncomfortable with this, so please lessen it.

BE CONSIDERATE OF OTHERS

Most rules are bendable, but once someone tells you to stop, stop immediately to avoid arguments.

PING ONLY WHEN NECESSARY

Nobody likes a phone that annoys your ears with pings. Lessen pinging, and ping the correct roles only.

DO NOT SPAM

Spamming may be okay if there's nobody there, but spam may flood the chat. 10 lines of similar content is considered spam.

USE COMMON SENSE

If a rule isn't stated here, that doesn't mean it's automatically allowed. If you think that it's wrong, don't do it unless you ask.
